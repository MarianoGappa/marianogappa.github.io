We saved $50k/y with a tiny Go microservice coded in a Hackathon

Apr 2, 2018
1 minute read

I’ve written a blogpost for Movio about how in the past few weeks we’ve rolled out a Go microservice called “KIB” to production, which reduced a huge portion of the infrastructure necessary for Movio’s main product. In doing this, we saved considerable AWS bills, after-hours and office-hours support bills, significantly simplified our architecture and made our product 80% faster on average. We wrote KIB on a Hackathon.

The actual blogpost is on the Movio blog: https://movio.co/en/blog/saving-money-with-Hackathon-project/
